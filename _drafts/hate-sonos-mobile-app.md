In a timely follow-up to my 2021 post [Things I hate about the Sonos desktop app](/posts/things-i-hate-about-the-sonos-app/)

* Laggy as fuuuck
* Sometimes takes multiple taps to register
* No notification tray card thing
* Doesn't remember where you were - always boots you back to the painfully slow to load home screen. Sometimes when the thing you chose to play is still playing.
* Services icons are in an irritaitng horizontal scroll when they could just be in a grid. Also, they have no text labels.
* Collapsed now playing view shows volume control, but I'd prefer a progress bar.
* Progress bar and volume bar look identical.
* View All in search results doesn't let you actually view all, it just scrolls the service lozenges.
